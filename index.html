About EAAA

Leading excellence in arthroscopic surgery across East Africa

Our Mission

The East African Arthroscopy Association (EAAA) is dedicated to advancing the field of arthroscopic surgery through education, research, and the promotion of best practices among orthopedic surgeons throughout East Africa.

Our Vision

To be the premier organization for arthroscopic surgery education and innovation in East Africa, ensuring patients receive the highest quality minimally invasive orthopedic care.

500+ Members
25+ Years of Excellence
10,000+ Procedures

Comprehensive arthroscopic and orthopedic solutions

Knee Arthroscopy
